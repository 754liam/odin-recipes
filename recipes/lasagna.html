Lasagna Recipe

Description

A lasagna is a baked Italian dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù (meat sauce), béchamel sauce, and cheese, typically ricotta and mozzarella. These layers are assembled and then baked until the pasta is tender, the sauces are bubbly, and the cheese is melted and golden brown. It's a rich and comforting casserole often enjoyed as a hearty main course.

Ingredients
  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings of your liking
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodles
Steps
  1. Prepare the meat sauce.
  2. Cook Lasagna noodles
  3. Assemble the cheese mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna.
  6. Rest and serve.
