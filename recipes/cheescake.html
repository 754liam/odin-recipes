Cheescake Recipe

Description

A cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert consisting of one or more layers, with the main and thickest layer being a mixture of soft, fresh cheese (typically cream cheese), eggs, and sugar. This filling usually rests upon a crust made from crushed cookies (like graham crackers) or sometimes pastry. Baked until set and often chilled, cheesecakes offer a delightful balance of tangy, sweet, and velvety textures.

Ingredients
  • Cream cheese
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Lemon zest or juice
  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Melted butter
Steps
  1. Prepare the crust.
  2. Make the filling.
  3. Assemble and bake.
  4. Chill and serve.
