Cheescake Recipe

Description

A sundae is a classic dessert featuring scoops of ice cream, typically vanilla, crowned with a variety of sweet toppings. Common embellishments include sauces like chocolate fudge or caramel, whipped cream, chopped nuts, and a maraschino cherry. It's a delightful and customizable treat, perfect for satisfying a sweet tooth.

Ingredients
  • Ice Cream
  • Syrup of choice: Chocolate, strawberry, caramel, etc
  • Whipped Cream
  • Topping of choice: maraschino cherry, chopped nuts, sprinkles, etc
Steps
  1. Scoop the ice cream into a container (glass, bowl, etc).
  2. Add the syrup on the ice cream.
  3. Top it with whipped cream.
  4. Apply the toping(s).
